# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2026

## Overview
RVC Native is a privacy-first, on-device voice conversion application powered by MLX Swift. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal data.** All processing happens locally on your device.

### What We Don't Collect:
- ❌ Personal information
- ❌ Audio recordings
- ❌ Usage analytics
- ❌ Crash reports
- ❌ Device identifiers

### What Stays on Your Device:
- ✅ All audio files you import or record
- ✅ All voice models you download
- ✅ All conversion results
- ✅ App settings and preferences

## Permissions
The app requests the following permissions:
- **Microphone**: Only for recording audio within the app
- **File Access**: Only for importing/exporting audio and model files

These permissions are used exclusively for app functionality and never for data collection.

## Third-Party Services
RVC Native does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device in the app's sandboxed container. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the app.
